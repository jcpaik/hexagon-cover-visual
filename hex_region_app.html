Hexagon region explorer

Drag each pᵢ along edge eᵢ=[Vᵢ,Vᵢ₊₁]. The app shades the union of the six regions Rᵢ inside the regular hexagon H. Here V₆=V₀.

Click Vᵢ to show only the boundary of Rᵢ. Click pᵢ to show the boundaries of Rᵢ and Rᵢ₊₁. Click elsewhere to clear boundaries.

The dashed triangle is the minimum-side equilateral triangle with first inward side-normal angle θ containing the currently uncovered part of H.
