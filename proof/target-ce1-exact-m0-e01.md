# Target: CE1 C-triangle, exact midpoint subset {M0}, edge e01

Use `common-half-skeleton-guide.md` as the complete context.  Solve the
normalized C-triangle target:
\[
T_C\text{ contains exactly }M_0\text{ among }M_0,\dots,M_5,
\]
and \(T_C\) has positive-length overlap with exactly one hexagon boundary edge,
namely \(e_{0,1}\).

Let
\[
T_C\cap e_{0,1}=[s_{01},t_{01}]
\]
in the edge parameter.  Find all maximal feasible intervals under interval
inclusion.  Do not maximize only interval length; maximality is by containment
of the interval itself.

Give symbolic inequalities, the maximal-interval derivation, boundary cases and degeneracies, and an independent numerical verification plan.
